'What's the matter with you, boy?' old Spencer said. He said it pretty tough, too, for him. 'How many subjects did you carry this term?' 'Five, sir.' 'Five. And how many are you failing in?' 'Four.' I moved my ass a little bit on the bed. It was the hardest bed I ever sat on. 'I passed English all right,' I said, 'because I had all that Beowulf and Lord Randal My Son stuff when I was at the Whooton School. I mean I didn't have to do any work in English at all hardly, except write compositions once in a while.' He wasn't even listening.
